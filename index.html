VALMIKI EDUSHOP

Interact Club of Valmiki successfully co-hosting an exciting event, "The EduShop," on August 23, 2024, Friday at the Valmiki College Hall. This event, hosted by the Scholastic Foundation, was a dynamic training session designed to equip our members with essential skills such as proposal writing, debate, public speaking, minute writing, and more. The session will be expertly led by Rtr. Amrit Dhakal, a respected past President of our club, along with his experienced team.
